How to lodge a grievance on pgportal - video

The grievances registered on PGPORTAL are forwarded to the concerned Ministries/Departments/State Governments/UTs. You can register your grievance there, and they will forward it to the concerned department under the Department of Telecommunication. You need to register an account first on the website before posting a grievance.

How to lodge a grievance on Directorate of Consumer Affairs & Fair Business Practices, WB

You can also post your grievance on Consumer Affairs Department's Website for a mediation. Here also you need to register an account first. Read this PDF to understand how to lodge a complaint.
